# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Focus on project outcomes and technical collaboration.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, discrimination, or hateful language
- trolling, insults, or personal attacks
- publishing private information without explicit permission
- conduct inappropriate for a professional environment

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to maintainers.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject content and contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
